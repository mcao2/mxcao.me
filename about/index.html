About

Hey there!

I’m Mengxin and I’m a software engineer at TuSimple.

Before that, I graduated from Master of Computational Data Science (MCDS), School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in 2020. My advisors are Prof. Eric Nyberg and Prof. Majd Sakr. I was a member of Language Technologies Institute and Parallel Data Lab.

I have a keen interest in databases, cloud computing, large-scale distributed systems, storage systems, and networking. I am passionate about tackling challenges in these areas. This website serves as a platform for me to share my thoughts and explorations.
